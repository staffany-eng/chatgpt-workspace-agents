# KNS Knowledge Materials

Use this reference for repo-approved KNS `Knowledge` material rows. It mirrors the one-sheet nurture material registry schema, but the live Google Sheet remains read-only runtime context through `read_nurture_material_registry`. HubSpot remains source of truth for account facts.

## Material Registry Shape

Minimum row mapping: `material_id`, `status`, `title`, `kns_pillar`, `message_hook`, `source_evidence_url`, `asset_url`, and `owner`. These rows are case-study proof, so `category = case_study` and `kns_pillar = Knowledge`. Rows with `status = needs-check` should not be treated as transcript-verified until captions, ASR, or approved subtitles exist.
