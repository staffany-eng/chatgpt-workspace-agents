# Launch Priority Materials

Source: 2026 Product Launch SOP and launch Slack brief.

## Priority Definitions

| Priority | Meaning | Examples |
| --- | --- | --- |
| P1 | New chargeable module | PayrollAny, HRAny |
| P2 | New feature or significant upgrade to a core flow | Dynamic QR, payment workflow |
| P3 | Small flow upgrade to an existing feature | Wall like list |
| P4 | Small UI/UX change | Leave calculation tooltip |

## Material Mapping

| Material | P1 | P2 | P3 | P4 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Help article | Required | Required | Required only if user behavior changes | Depends |
| Release notes | Required | Required | Usually required | Depends |

## Out Of Scope

- Changelog / What's New is covered by the release notes step for this workflow.
- WhatsApp Community messages are decoupled from Product Marketing workflow scope and should not be drafted, evaluated, or tracked here.
- Rev / CS / PS enablement, webinar, website, pricing, blog, social, PR, and sales decks are separate handoffs unless a teammate explicitly starts another workflow.
