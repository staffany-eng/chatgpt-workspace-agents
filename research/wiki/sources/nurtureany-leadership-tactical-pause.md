## What They Said

- Sales leaders own the outbound funnel end to end: Leads -> Activities -> QO -> QO Met -> Deals.
- The current policy replaces raw 450 activity points with activity hygiene first.
- Each AE should protect 150 priority accounts and cover 120 of them weekly.
- Daily activity rhythm includes 30 WhatsApp nurturing touches and double taps, plus connected-call discipline where the channel fits.
- Clean leads and QOs require industry, headcount, current solution, contract end date, and a buying-relevant contact.
- Warm activity proof requires a photo or selfie, follow-up WhatsApp, and HubSpot meeting log.
- Friday is a correction point for missed coverage, activity quality, and funnel-quality gaps.
