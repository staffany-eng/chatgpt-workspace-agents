## What They Said

- CCC means Connect, Curiosity, Convert, and Fallback; strong outreach is relevant, specific, and asks for a clear next step.
- CBANT means Connect, Budget, Authority, Need, and Timeline; qualification should map value, approval path, real pain, and dates.
- Pre-demo planning needs static facts, research, hypothesis, current tools, contract end date, and game plans A and B.
- Pre-demo nurturing earns the meeting through Curiosity, Credibility, Context, and K/N/S value over a 14-to-18-day five-touch rhythm.
- A value-driven demo is not a product walkthrough; it should be consultative, contextual, benefit-led, and tied to before/after value.
- Post-demo follow-up earns a decision, identifies the real blocker or loss reason, and uses a structured follow-up cadence.
- Current instructor copies and current rubrics outrank archived, old, copy, or trainee materials.
